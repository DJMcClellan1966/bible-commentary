## Understanding Ephesians 2:8

**The Verse:**

For by grace have ye been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God;

**What This Means:**

This verse calls us to trust - not in our own understanding or abilities, but in God Himself. Trust is not blind faith, but confidence in the character of the One who is trustworthy. It is not about having all the answers, but about knowing the One who does.
