## Understanding Proverbs 3:5-6

**The Verse:**

Trust in the Lord with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding. In all thy ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct thy paths.

**What This Means:**

This verse speaks to the heart of trust, thine, heart;. It calls us to a relationship of trust, not based on our understanding but on God's character. The words here are not mere information, but an invitation to relationship - to know God, to trust Him, to experience His love and peace in our daily lives.
